The Tube to Fluency

Learn English Like You're in the Movies!

Imagine stepping into your favorite movie or TV show as one of the characters. That's how our English learning method works – it's like karaoke, but instead of songs, you act out fun scenes from shows you love.

Want to give it a try?

Why it Works:

  • Learn to speak like real people do
  • Remember words and phrases naturally through context
  • No stress about mistakes – you can always try again
  • Practice English in a fun, game-like way
  • Repeat your favorite scenes as many times as you want, at your own pace
  • Track your progress and share your success with friends or teachers

How to Start:

  • Visit www.tube2fluency.com
  • Sign up for free
  • Choose playlist “Let’s start with…”
  • Pick a scene from your favorite movie or show
  • Watch it with subtitles
  • Explore the text with our smart assistant
  • Create flashcards for new words
  • Choose your character and start practicing
  • Practice saying the lines
  • Adjust speed and hints to your comfort
  • Master the scene through exercises
  • Level up by increasing speed and removing hints
  • Save your progress and share your achievements
